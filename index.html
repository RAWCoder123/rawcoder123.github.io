What Mise does

Everything that runs behind a great restaurant — automated.

Mise connects to your POS, supplier accounts, and schedules to give you a self-running operation.

🛒

Autonomous Purchasing

Mise monitors your par levels, sales velocity, and supplier lead times, then automatically places orders before you run out — negotiating the best prices across your approved vendor list.

  • Auto-reorder based on real usage data
  • Price-compare across suppliers instantly
  • Approve or override with one tap
📦

Live Inventory Tracking

Know exactly what you have, what you're burning through, and what you're wasting — updated in real time as orders come in and deliveries arrive.

  • Automatic depletion from POS sales
  • Waste logging and trend alerts
  • Shelf-life and expiry warnings
⚙️

Operations Intelligence

From staff scheduling to prep sheets, Mise turns your historical data into actionable operational playbooks that update every week based on what's actually happening in your kitchen.

  • Demand-based prep quantity recommendations
  • Labor cost optimization suggestions
  • Vendor performance scorecards
📊

Financial Command Center

See your food cost percentage, prime cost, and margin by menu item in a single dashboard — with AI-generated explanations and recommended actions.

  • Daily P&L snapshots
  • Menu engineering insights
  • Spend analytics by category
🔔

Proactive Alerts

Mise watches for anomalies and flags them before they become problems — a supplier price spike, an unusual waste day, or a prep item running critically low.

  • Price anomaly detection
  • Low-stock and over-order warnings
  • Delivered via SMS, email, or in-app
🔗

Integrates With Everything

Mise connects to the tools you already use — Toast, Square, Clover, US Foods, Sysco, and 40+ other POS and supplier platforms.

  • Set up in under 30 minutes
  • No disruption to existing workflows
  • Open API for custom integrations
How it works

Up and running in 30 minutes. Hands-off after that.

01

Connect your systems

Link your POS and supplier accounts. Mise pulls your historical sales, current inventory, and existing order patterns to build your kitchen's baseline.

02

Review your profile

Mise generates a restaurant profile with your approved vendors, par levels, and cost targets. You review and approve — or adjust anything to match how you like to run.

03

Mise takes the wheel

From here, Mise monitors your inventory in real time, places orders automatically, flags issues before they become problems, and delivers a daily operations briefing to your phone.

04

You stay in control

Every automated action is visible and overridable. Mise is your AI operations manager — always working, always transparent, never opaque.
