The problem

Built for operators who don't have time. Not analysts who want dashboards.

Most restaurants are over-ordering, under-ordering, and manually managing everything in between — losing money to waste and losing sales to empty shelves. All while running service.

Mise doesn't give you another system to learn. It watches your sales, tracks your inventory, and tells you exactly what to prep, order, and buy — in plain language, every day.

What Mise does

Four things. Done automatically, every day.

No setup complexity. No learning curve. Mise speaks your language — ingredients, orders, and prep — not metrics and modules.

📈

Demand Intelligence

Mise reads your sales history and spots the patterns you don't have time to notice — which items spike on weekends, how weather affects covers, when a slow week follows a big one.

  • Predicts what you'll need before you run out
  • Accounts for seasonality and local events
  • Gets smarter the longer you use it
📦

Inventory Optimization

Know exactly what's on hand, what's being used, and what's about to expire — without counting every shelf manually. Mise prevents over-ordering and catches waste before it happens.

  • Real-time depletion from POS sales
  • Shelf-life and expiry alerts
  • Waste tracking with trend summaries
🛒

Purchase Recommendations

Every day, Mise generates exact supplier orders — the right quantities, from the right vendors, timed to arrive exactly when you need them. You approve in one tap or let it run.

  • Exact quantities based on real usage data
  • Best-price comparison across your vendors
  • Approve, adjust, or skip with one tap
✅

Order Execution

Once you approve, Mise sends the order directly to your supplier — formatted exactly how they want it. No phone calls, no faxes, no re-keying into a portal.

  • Direct supplier messaging (email, portal, EDI)
  • Confirmation tracking and delivery alerts
  • Full order history in one place
🔁 Learning Loop — Mise improves with every order. The more it runs your kitchen, the more accurate its recommendations become.
