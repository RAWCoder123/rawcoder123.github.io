What Mise Does

Three things. Done automatically, every day.

No dashboards to learn. No new workflows to build. Mise speaks your language — ingredients, prep, orders.

Real-time depletion from your POS. Know what's on hand, what's expiring, and what's being wasted — without counting every shelf manually.
